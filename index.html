Who We Are

Built on Purpose.
Powered by AI.

Navmarg Consulting was founded on a single conviction: every organisation deserves a clear path through the complexity of AI adoption. "Navmarg" — drawing from Sanskrit roots meaning new path — reflects our role as a trusted guide for enterprises navigating intelligent transformation.

We design, build, and deploy AI-enabled SaaS products that address the most persistent pain-points in project delivery, customer engagement, data storytelling, and team productivity. Simultaneously, we equip leaders and practitioners through world-class training programmes in AI Product Management and Agentic AI Development.

Headquartered at Bagmane World Tech Park, Bangalore, our team of engineers, AI researchers, and domain experts partners with clients across BFSI, healthcare, retail, and professional services.
